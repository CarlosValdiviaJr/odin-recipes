Protein Pasta

Description

This is a healthy high protein pasta that has minemal ingredients and is easy to make

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 4 cups bone broth
  • 1 box Barilla protein pasta
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • Seasonings: garlic powder, chili powder, paprika, salt, and pepper

Steps

  1. Dice 1 onion
  2. On medium to high heat, spray pot with avocado oil, and add the onion
  3. Add 2lbs of ground beef
  4. Add 1 teaspoon of garlic powder, 1 tablespoon of chili powder, 1/2 tablespoon of paprika, and 1 teaspoon of salt and pepper
  5. After about 8 minutes add 3 tablespoons of tomato paste, and mix
  6. Add 4 cups of bone broth and turn heat up to high
  7. Once broth is boiling add 1 box of Barilla protien pasta, reduce heat to medium
  8. Once pasta is completely cooked, add 2 cupps of cheese, and turn heat to low
  9. Mix to completion
